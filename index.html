Quack is an application that allows you to quickly survey your friends with questions that you want to ask them. Submit questions and possible answers and select which friends to send them to. Answer friends' questions with the click of a button. After questions are answered, they disappeared so that your inbox doesn't get cluttered. You can view results of questions you have asked and how many people chose each answer. Push notifications allow friends to see when you've asked them a question. Use this application for a quick, easy method of gathering the opinions of friends and colleagues.

Ask your friends questions • Type out a question and 1-4 possible answers • Select who you want to ask (choose from friends who have the app) • Push notifications notify friends that you've asked them a question

Answer questions asked of you from friends • View questions in an organized manner • Select from up to four answers chosen by asker • Clear out questions from inbox by answering them

View results of questions you've asked • Organized table of results shows questions you've asked • Expand questions revealing charts showing how many chose each answer • Gather feedback quickly to make more informed decisions
